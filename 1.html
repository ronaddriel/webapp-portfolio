Overview

This project is a visualizer for Beabadoobee's "Glue Song". The video features a full collage animation created using Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, and Illustrator. The design draws inspiration from the song's dreamy, nostalgic vibe, combining a mix of vintage textures, Filipino culture elements, and surreal imagery to create a visual experience that complements the music's warm and whimsical tone.
