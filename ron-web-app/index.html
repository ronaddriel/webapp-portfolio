Get to know me and the stuff that I love doing!

I'm currently a senior at Lehman College, majoring in Computer Graphics & Imaging (CGI). I have a deep passion for creating art through digital tools — whether I'm using my computer or sketching ideas on my iPad!

My creative work spans across graphic design, illustration, UI/UX design, and a bit of front-end development. As a jack of all trades, I have experience in both 2D and 3D art, along with a strong background in typography, scripting, and light video editing.

I'm always eager to push myself, refine my skills, and explore new techniques. Creativity is my way of connecting ideas and bringing them to life.
