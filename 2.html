Overview

Treat is a conceptual boutique dog treat brand designed to make pet snacks feel playful, stylish, and premium. Inspired by retro grocery packaging, risograph textures, and indie food branding, the brand combines bold typography, earthy flavor palettes, and expressive ingredient visuals to create packaging that feels both nostalgic and modern.

Deliverables

  • - Brand Identity
  • - Packaging Design
  • - Logo Variations
  • - Product Posters

To create a rustic and nostalgic look, the brand utilizes grainy textures, halftone graphics, and warm earthy tones. These elements help create a playful personality while reinforcing the handcrafted feel.
